So, how do they work?

  • Survival Horror
  • Thrillers
  • Jumpscare Simulators

Survival Horror

Mostly big-budget games that have an intricate, well-thought-out story.

Survival Horror

Characterized by gameplay elements like resource management and complex gameplay mechanics.

Thrillers

A well-planned slow-burn of fear analogous to a roller coaster of emotions.

Thrillers

Usually characterized by intense action followed by a reprieve, with limited self-defense options.

Jumpscare Simulators

Made famous by Five Nights at Freddy's.

Jumpscare Simulators

Consist of minimal gameplay, and lots of opportunities for jumpscares.

Why would you want to experience these things?

In today's society, people are very safe.

They often yearn for a thrill.

These are the things they're looking for:

  • Cheap thrills to get their heart rates up. (FNAF/Slender)
  • Extended and drawn-out thrills. (Alien/Amnesia)
  • Puzzle aspects and resource management. (RE2R/Dead Space)
